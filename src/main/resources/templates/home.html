Cryptopad

Cryptopad is an encrypted notepad/journal written in java. It provides users with an easy and seamless writing experience while providing security and privacy. Users can create, save, and edit text documents and encrypt them at their own discretion. Choose from a range of ciphers to keep what you want private, private.
